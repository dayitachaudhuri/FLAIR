About the Lab

Our group broadly works in the areas of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) with a focus on developing robust, fair, and interpretable AI systems. From the outset, the lab is envisioned to be diverse, transparent, friendly and highly collaborative.

We are grateful to Schmidt Sciences, Google Research, Adobe Inc., Microsoft Research, National Payments Corporations of India (NPCI), and Pratiksha Trust for generously supporting our research.

If you are interested in joining our group, email Danish.

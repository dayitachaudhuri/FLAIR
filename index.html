About Us
Research Areas

Our group broadly works in the areas of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML). Our research focuses on developing robust, fair, and interpretable AI systems with applications in Natural Language Processing. From the outset, the lab is envisioned to be diverse, transparent, friendly and highly collaborative.

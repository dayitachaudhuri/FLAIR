About FLAIR Lab

CDS, IISc

Our group broadly works in the areas of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML). From the outset, the lab is envisioned to be diverse, transparent, friendly and highly collaborative. We are grateful to Schmidt Sciences, Google Research, Adobe Inc., Microsoft Research, National Payments Corporations of India (NPCI) and Pratiksha Trust for generously supporting our group's research.

Research Areas

Our research focuses on developing robust, fair, and interpretable AI systems with applications in Natural Language Processing.

Robustness in NLP

Developing neural models that are robust to adversarial attacks and distribution shifts. We focus on understanding vulnerabilities in current NLP systems and creating defenses.

  • Adversarial robustness
  • Out-of-distribution generalization
  • Certified defenses
Fairness in ML

Ensuring machine learning systems are fair and unbiased across different demographic groups. We develop methods to detect and mitigate bias in AI systems.

  • Bias detection and mitigation
  • Fairness metrics
  • Algorithmic auditing
Interpretability

Making AI systems more transparent and interpretable to humans. We develop methods to explain model decisions and understand their behavior.

  • Model explanations
  • Attention analysis
  • Probing studies
